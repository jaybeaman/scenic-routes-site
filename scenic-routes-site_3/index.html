Scenic Routes Community Bicycle Center

Slow is Forever

We're a scrappy, small, independent, community-focused utilitarian bicycle repair shop in San Francisco that primarily restores and rebuilds old bikes for commuters and travelers. We believe the bicycle is a tool of freedom, social equality, and happiness for everyone.

Stay in the loop
